1. Leave Entitlement (per calendar year)

  Type                    Days    Carry Forward    Encashment
  ---------------------   ----    -------------    ----------

  Casual Leave (CL)       8       No               No
  Sick Leave (SL)         6       No               No
  Maternity Leave         26 weeks (as per Maternity Benefit Act)
  Paternity Leave         5 days
  Bereavement Leave       3 days (immediate family)
  Marriage Leave          3 days (one-time, within first year of employment)

2. Leave Application Process

  a) All leave must be applied through the HRMS portal at least 3 working days in advance
  b) Emergency/sick leave: inform the reporting manager via call/message before 10:00 AM
  c) Medical certificate required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days
  d) Earned leave for more than 5 consecutive days requires manager + HR approval
  e) Leave during notice period is generally not allowed unless pre-approved

3. Attendance and Work Hours

  Standard work hours: 9 hours/day (including 1 hour lunch break)
  Core hours: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  Flexible window: 8:30 AM to 10:30 AM (start) / 5:30 PM to 7:30 PM (end)
  Minimum monthly attendance: 22 working days

4. Work From Home (WFH) Policy

  - Maximum 2 WFH days per week (subject to project requirements)
  - WFH must be recorded in the attendance system before 10:00 AM
  - Employees on WFH must be reachable during core hours
  - New joiners (first 3 months) are expected to work from office full-time
  - Client-specific WFH restrictions may apply

5. Overtime and Compensatory Off

  - Overtime is applicable only for employees below manager grade
  - Weekend work requires prior approval from the delivery manager
  - Compensatory off must be availed within 30 days of the overtime
  - If not availed within 30 days, it lapses automatically
