5. Notes

- All rates are exclusive of GST (18%)
- Bench period cost is absorbed by the company for up to 30 days
- For fixed-price projects, apply a 15% margin on top of T&M rates
- Volume discount: 5% for engagements exceeding 10 consultants
- Night-shift differential: +15% on base rate
- Weekend/holiday work: +25% on base rate
- Rates are revised annually in April; mid-year revisions require VP approval
