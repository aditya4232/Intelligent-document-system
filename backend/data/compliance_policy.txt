Compliance and Background Verification Policy
Company: Aditya Consulting Services Pvt. Ltd.
Effective Date: 1 January 2026
Version: 3.1

1. Pre-Employment Background Verification

All candidates must complete background verification before their start date. No exceptions.

1.1 Required Checks
- Identity verification: Aadhaar, PAN and passport (if applicable)
- Address verification through third-party agency
- Educational qualification check (10th, 12th, graduation, post-graduation)
- Previous employment verification for the last three employers
- Criminal background check through police verification
- Credit history check for finance-domain roles only

1.2 Timeline
- BGV must be initiated within 2 working days of offer acceptance
- Reports are expected within 10â€“15 working days
- Any discrepancy must be escalated to the HR Manager within 24 hours

1.3 Failure Handling
If a candidate fails the background check:
  a) Notify the candidate in writing within 24 hours
  b) Provide a copy of the report upon request
  c) Allow 7 working days for the candidate to dispute findings
  d) Escalate to the legal team if criminal records are found
  e) Revoke the offer letter and notify the client within 48 hours

2. PF and ESIC Compliance

2.1 Provident Fund
- PF is mandatory for all employees earning basic salary up to INR 15,000/month
- Employer contribution: 12% of basic + DA
- Employee contribution: 12% of basic + DA
- UAN must be generated within 7 days of joining

2.2 ESIC (Employee State Insurance Corporation)
- Applicable for employees with gross salary up to INR 21,000/month
- Employer contribution: 3.25%
- Employee contribution: 0.75%
- ESI card must be issued within 15 working days

2.3 TDS Compliance
- TDS to be deducted at source as per applicable tax slab
- Form 16 to be issued by 15 June each year
- Quarterly TDS returns (24Q) to be filed on time

3. Work Authorization

3.1 Indian Nationals
- Aadhaar and PAN are mandatory
- Voter ID or driving license as supplementary proof

3.2 Foreign Nationals
- Valid work visa (Employment Visa)
- FRRO registration within 14 days of arrival
- Work permit verification with the Ministry of Home Affairs

4. Annual Compliance Calendar

  Q1 (Apr-Jun): PF annual returns, TDS Q4 filing, Form 16 issuance
  Q2 (Jul-Sep): Half-yearly compliance review, ESIC contribution reconciliation
  Q3 (Oct-Dec): ESI renewal, mid-year audit
  Q4 (Jan-Mar): Annual BGV re-verification for critical roles, PF withdrawal settlements
