Employee Exit and Full & Final Settlement Process
Aditya Consulting Services Pvt. Ltd.
Version: 1.3 | Updated: January 2026

1. Resignation Process

  a) Submit resignation through the HRMS portal
  b) Notice period: 30 days for below-manager grades, 60 days for managers and above
  c) Notice period buyout: permitted with manager and HR approval; deduction is calculated
     as (monthly gross salary / 30) x remaining notice days
  d) Manager to acknowledge resignation within 2 working days
  e) HR to schedule an exit interview within the last week

2. Handover and Knowledge Transfer

  - Prepare a handover document covering:
    * Current project status and pending tasks
    * Access credentials and admin accounts
    * Documentation and codebase walkthroughs
    * Key contacts (client and internal)
  - Knowledge transfer sessions must be completed at least 5 days before the last day
  - Delivery manager to sign off on handover completion

3. Asset Return Checklist

  [ ] Laptop and charger
  [ ] ID badge and access cards
  [ ] Parking pass (if issued)
  [ ] Client-provided equipment (monitors, peripherals)
  [ ] Library books or reference materials
  [ ] Company-issued mobile phone (if applicable)

4. Access Revocation

  - Corporate email: deactivated on the last working day
  - VPN and remote access: revoked on the last working day
  - JIRA, Confluence, Git: access removed within 24 hours
  - Building access: badge deactivated on the last working day
  - Client systems: access removal confirmation from client IT team

5. Full and Final Settlement

  Components included:
    + Salary for days worked in the last month
    + Earned leave encashment
    + Bonus (pro-rated, if applicable)
    + Gratuity (for employees with 5+ years of service)
    + Reimbursements pending

  Deductions:
    - Notice period shortfall (if notice not fully served)
    - Outstanding advances or loans
    - Asset recovery charges (if any asset not returned)
    - Tax deductions (TDS on final settlement)

  Timeline: Full and final settlement is processed within 45 working days of the last
  working day, provided all clearances are received.

6. Experience Letter and Relieving Letter

  - Relieving letter: issued on the last working day (subject to clearance)
  - Experience letter: issued within 7 working days of exit clearance
  - Both letters are sent to the registered email and available on HRMS
